About Dailywin

Our mission is to help you celebrate small victories every day.

Our Story

Dailywin was born from a simple idea: small wins compound into big achievements. We believe that tracking your daily progress, no matter how small, is the key to building lasting habits and achieving your goals.

Too many habit trackers are complicated, overwhelming, and make you feel bad when you miss a day. We wanted to create something different – an app that celebrates your wins, understands that consistency beats perfection, and takes just 2 minutes of your day.

Our Mission

Help millions of people build better habits through daily reflection and celebration of small wins.

Our Philosophy

Progress over perfection. Every small step forward is worth celebrating, even on the tough days.

Our Promise

We'll always keep Dailywin simple, beautiful, and focused on what matters – your growth.

Why Dailywin?

Here's what makes us different:

  • Simple by design: No complicated features or overwhelming dashboards. Just what you need.
  • Quick check-ins: Morning and evening reflections take just 2 minutes each.
  • Beautiful insights: Visualize your progress with charts and patterns that actually make sense.
  • Flexible habits: Track habits your way – with timers, toggles, or simple tags.
  • Privacy first: Your data stays on your device. We don't track or sell your information.
  • 100% free: All features are available for free. No premium walls, no subscriptions.

The Science Behind It

Research shows that daily reflection and habit tracking significantly improve goal achievement. By taking just a few minutes each day to check in with yourself, you:

  • Build self-awareness about your patterns and behaviors
  • Increase accountability through consistent tracking
  • Celebrate wins, which releases dopamine and reinforces positive behavior
  • Identify what works (and what doesn't) through data-driven insights
