Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. DailyWin stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on any servers.

Introduction

Welcome to DailyWin. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

DailyWin stores all your data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Daily Check-ins: Your mood ratings, energy levels, sleep data, and daily notes
  • Habits: Your habit tracking data and progress
  • Todos: Your tasks, reminders, and completion status
  • Journal Entries: Your personal journal entries and reflections
  • Quick Wins: Your daily challenge completions
  • Profile Information: Your name and profile picture (if provided)
  • App Settings: Your preferences including theme selection

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information from you
  • We do not transmit your data to any servers
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Display your progress and insights
  • Send local notifications for reminders
  • Generate your daily and weekly reports

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device
  • Your data remains on your device at all times
  • We do not have access to your data
  • You can export your data at any time from the Settings screen
  • You can delete all your data from the Settings screen

Permissions

DailyWin may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders for habits and todos
  • Storage: To export/import your data backups
  • Camera (optional): To take photos for journal entries or profile picture
  • Photo Library (optional): To select images for journal entries or profile picture

Data Backup and Export

  • You can export your data as a JSON file from the Settings screen
  • Exported files contain all your app data
  • You can import previously exported data to restore your information
  • Backups are stored locally on your device

Children's Privacy

DailyWin does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Export: You can export your data at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all your data from Settings
  • Control: You have full control over what data you enter

Third-Party Services

DailyWin does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. All functionality is self-contained within the app.
